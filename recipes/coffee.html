Odin's Instant Coffee

Description

Make instant coffee that doesn't taste burnt. Good taste shouldn't require you to splurge.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp instant coffee powder/granules
  • A dash of cold water
  • 80 degrees celsius hot water

Steps

  1. Add cold water to a cup.
  2. Add in the coffee powder/granules and whisk with a spoon until it froths.
  3. Heat water to 80 degrees celsius or boil water in a kettle and let it sit for five minutes.
  4. Pour hot water in the cup and stir. Add sugar or cream to taste.
