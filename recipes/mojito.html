Mojito

Description

A refreshing, lime-mint mojito with simple syrup. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 10 mint leaves
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 2 tbsp simple syrup
  • 10 oz sparkling water
  • Lime slices (optional)

Steps

  1. Add mint leaves and lime juice in a glass and muddle them.
  2. Add the simple syrup.
  3. Add the sparkling water.
  4. Garnish with lime slices, and add salt to the rim.
