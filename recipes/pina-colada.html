Piña Colada

Description

A sip of paradise awaits you in this non-alcoholic piña colada. Where the tropical flavours transport you straight to Ibiza!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups pineapple juice
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 1 cup ice
  • Pineapple wedges for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Pour the pineapple juice and coconut milk in a blender.
  2. Add the ice and blend until smooth, for about 60 seconds.
  3. Divide between 3 glasses.
  4. Garnish with pineapple wedges, if desired.
