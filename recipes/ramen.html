Odin's Ramen

Description

A flavourful miso broth ramen enriched with probiotic ingredients like organic kimchi, freshly boiled eggs, and a pop of green cabbage.

Ingredients

  • 300g ramen noodles
  • 1 large hardboiled egg
  • 1/4 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1/4 cup kimchi
  • 1 tbsp miso paste
  • 1.5 cup chicken stock
  • 2 poached chicken breasts
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • Cucumber rounds for garnish

Steps

  1. Soak ramen noodles in water as directed by the package.
  2. Fry cabbage in oil for 3 minutes in a medium saucepan and add stock.
  3. Bring to a boil and simmer, adding miso paste.
  4. Add chicken breasts, soy sauce, and egg.
  5. Stir occassionally for 10 minutes and take off the heat.
  6. Add kimchi and cucumber rounds, if desired.
