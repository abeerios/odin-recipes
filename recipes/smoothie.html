Smoothie

Description

This refreshing mango and berry smoothie is only three ingredients! It's vitamin-rich and creamy, perfect for a hot day out by the pool or a garden. Enjoy with a spritz of lime or a garnish of mint!

Ingredients

  • Frozen mango chunks
  • Fresh berries
  • Full fat milk
  • Mint or lime for garnish

Steps

  1. Add mango to the blender.
  2. Pour 1/2 cup milk.
  3. Slowly add the berries.
  4. Blend, and garnish with mint or lime.
