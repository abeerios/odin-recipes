Soup

Description

This is a fragrant, minestrone soup made using traditional Italian spices. The combination of tender chicken and fresh parsley, makes this a soup you'll cook for years to come.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts
  • Peeled tomato chunks
  • Chicken broth
  • Orecchiette
  • Mixed vegetables
  • Italian spices
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Slice the chicken breasts into even cubes.
  2. Add the chicken broth and bring to a boil.
  3. Add mixed vegetables and keep boiling for ten minutes.
  4. Stir in spices, adding salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Garnish with parsley and serve warm.
