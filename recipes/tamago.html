Odin's Onsen Tamago

Description

A quick way to poach eggs japanese-style in the microwave. No hot spring water necessary!

Ingredients

  • Water
  • 1 large refrigerated egg

Steps

  1. Crack the egg into a micorwave-safe bowl.
  2. Add water around the edges and microwave for 3 minutes.
  3. Carefully scoop the egg with a spoon and drain the water.
  4. Garnish with black pepper or shichimi togarashi.
