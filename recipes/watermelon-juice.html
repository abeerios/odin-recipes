Watermelon Juice

Description

This 3-ingredient watermelon juice is extra-easy to make and can be the base for many other, equally tasty drinks!

Ingredients

  • 1 small seedless watermelon
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • Pinch of sea salt

Steps

  1. Scoop flesh out of watermelon and cut into cubes.
  2. Add lime juice and a pinch of sea salt.
  3. Blend for 30 seconds or until smooth.
  4. Chill and serve.
